Summer Hackathon

The pinnacle of our annual calendar, the Summer Hackathon is a premier 24-hour innovation challenge held right after the exam session (20-21 June). We invite students, young specialists, and IT enthusiasts to collaborate in a high-energy environment, solving real-world challenges alongside top-tier industry mentors.

XMAS Hackathon

Experience the unique blend of technical intensity and holiday spirit. Exclusively for the FAF community, this 24-hour hackathon takes place on the weekend before Christmas break. It’s more than a competition; it’s a celebration where our community comes together as a family to build, learn, and share the Christmas vibe.

An empowering IT conference and workshop series held on 9 March 2025, dedicated to bridging the gender gap in technology. She-novate provides a platform where all keynote speeches and technical workshops are led by successful women in the industry, offering inspiration, mentorship, and practical skill-building to the next generation of women in tech.

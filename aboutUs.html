Connecting Moldova's IT Community

Who we are

FAF is a student-led non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the gap between university education and the IT industry in Moldova. We foster a community where passion for technology meets practical application.

Events & Governance

We take pride in creating high-impact events like Hackathons, FAF Lectures, Workshops and others. These initiatives are made possible by our dedicated boards—groups of students who manage everything from logistics and finance to partnerships and marketing. Each event is a testament to the collaborative spirit of our community.
